Cocoon: RAG for Data Warehouse Tables

Cocoon thoroughly prepares your Data Warehouse (e.g., Snowflake, Big Query, SQL Server, DuckDB...) for RAG.
Offline, Cocoon helps profile, clean, and connect your tables into a lightweight catalog (SQL + YML).
Online, you can easily RAG your database for use cases like data discovery and report building.
