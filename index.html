Cocoon is a cross-team platform for creating and managing the next-gen data catalog, transforming large, messy enterprise data into GenAI-readable intelligence.

AI is Hard Because Data is Hard

If Humans Can't Understand Enterprise Data, Neither Can AI

Cocoon unlocks GenAI in 3 steps

Catalog for GenAI

Semantically describe your data

  • Detailed catalog in YML

    Describe your tables and files in detail. Summarize their relationships and connect them to the business.

  • Mostly in Natural Language

    Easy for LLMs to use. Friendly for humans to understand, verify and maintain.

  • Incorporate Enterprise Knowledge

    Encompasses existing enterprise knowledge: DWs, lakes, documents, dbt models, human feedback, etc.

Quality Monitoring

Continuously identify and resolve issues

  • Semantic Data Quality

    Profile data semantically. Alert issues about data ambiguity, semantic overlap, and business logics.

  • Human-In-the-Loop

    Seek clarification and verification from data providers. Explain the issues in natural language.

  • Continuous Monitoring

    Contextualize new data from the existing ones. Identify semantic inconsistency issues.

Data Preprocessing

Shape your data, easy for online query

  • Comprehensive Modeling in SQL

    Automatically write SQL for data modeling (casting, expanding JSON, SCD, normalization, etc.)

  • Data Pipelines in DBT

    Follow the existing best practices from DBT (CI/CD, incremental materialization, governance, etc.)

  • Testing and Maintenance

    Automatically suggest tests based on semantic understanding. Propose fixes to broken pipelines.
