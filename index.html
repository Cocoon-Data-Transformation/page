Cocoon

RAG for Large Data Warehouses

GenAI fails because enterprise data is large and messy with 1000+ tables.
Cocoon unlocks GenAI by building the RAG-ready Data Warehouse. Specifically, Cocoon helps you:

  • Thoroughly profile, clean, and document data with human verification
  • Leverage existing knowledge from pipelines, documents, and BI objects
  • Provide RAG for reliable GenAI apps like Pipeline Copilot and Self-Service BI

Complex Data Warehouse Tables

Cocoon handles messy data warehouses by cleaning, profiling, and documenting. It connects numerous tables through business concepts, preparing them for RAG.

Large Data Pipelines

Cocoon learns and semantically connects large data pipelines. It analyzes models and traces column lineages, transforming pipeline data into RAG-ready formats.
