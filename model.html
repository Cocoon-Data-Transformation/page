Data Modeling

Your data warehouses may have too many tables with convoluted logic that are difficult to navigate. Cocoon uses LLMs to capture the core concepts behind the tables, model the relationships among them and tell the story to help understanding.

Get Started

Open Source

Full Features

Cocoon is open-source. Try out Cocoon in Google Colab.

Cocoon connects to your data warehouses (e.g., Snowflake, Duckdb...) and uses LLMs (e.g., GPT-4, Claude-3, Gemini-Ultra, or your local LLMs) to standardize tables.
